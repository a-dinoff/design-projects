Both.
Senior Product Designer · Challenge prototype · 2026

A dinner app for couples who don't want to cook two meals.

One partner just went gluten-free, dairy-free, low-FODMAP, or GLP-1. The other didn't. This prototype shows one flow: from home screen to locked-in dinner in under 90 seconds, with a single recipe both people feel good about.

Audience
Cohabiting couples, 25–45, one partner with a new-ish dietary restriction (<12 mo).
Core journey
Tonight's dinner, both happy. Six screens, end-to-end.
Decision frequency
~4 weeknight dinners per couple per week.
North star
Both-happy meals per week per couple. Target: 4+ by week 4.
